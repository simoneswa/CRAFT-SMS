# CRAFT SMS - Parent Quickstart Guide
**Institutional Transparency & Monitoring**

Welcome to CRAFT SMS. This platform provides you with real-time visibility into your child's educational progress.

## 1. Accessing Your Account
- **Web Link**: Visit your school's unique portal (e.g., `yourschool.craftsms.com`).
- **Identity Verification**: Use the phone number or email registered during enrollment to log in.
- **PWA Installation**: Add CRAFT SMS to your mobile home screen for a seamless, app-like experience.

## 2. Monitoring Academic Progress
- **Grade Summary**: View the **Academic Overview** card to see current averages and recent grade distributions.
- **Attendance Alerts**: Monitor daily presence. If your child is marked 'Absent' or 'Late', you will see an immediate operational warning.

## 3. Financial Transparency
- **Payment Slips**: View all submitted and verified financial records in the **Finance** tab.
- **Verification Status**: Confirm that your tuition and fee payments have been officially cleared by the school business office.

## 4. Institutional Communication
- **Broadcasts**: Stay updated with school-wide announcements and emergency alerts.
- **Teacher Contact**: Receive direct updates regarding academic performance or behavior.

## 5. Security & Privacy
- **Secure Access**: Your data is isolated and only accessible to you and authorized school administrators.
- **Support**: If you have trouble accessing your account, please contact the school administration office.
