# CRAFT SMS - Institutional Onboarding Guide
**Operational Release**: v1.0.0

This guide outlines the process for initializing and configuring a new school tenant on the CRAFT SMS platform.

## 1. Tenant Initialization
- **Request Access**: Contact the CRAFT SMS Deployment Team to provision your unique subdomain (e.g., `yourschool.craftsms.com`).
- **Administrative Activation**: Follow the link in your activation email to set your secure password and initialize the School Admin profile.

## 2. Branding & Identity Configuration
- **Official Identity**: Navigate to **Settings > Institutional Branding**.
- **Logo Upload**: Provide a high-resolution version of your institutional logo (PNG/SVG).
- **Institutional Palette**: Select your primary and secondary brand colors to customize the dashboard theme.

## 3. Academic Foundation Setup
- **Academic Terms**: Define your semester dates and grading periods in **Academic > Terms**.
- **Institutional Structure**:
  1. Create **Subjects** (e.g., Mathematics, Science).
  2. Define **Classes** (e.g., Grade 10A, Grade 11B).
  3. Assign **Teachers** to subjects and classes.

## 4. Operational Rollout (Day 1)
- **Student Enrollment**: Utilize the **Enrollment Wizard** to import or manually register students.
- **Parent Linking**: Ensure every student profile is linked to at least one parent/guardian for automated notifications.
- **Attendance Initialization**: Train teachers to utilize the **Sync-Aware Attendance** module for real-time presence monitoring.

## 5. Support & Operational Continuity
- **Technical Support**: [support@craft-sms.com]
- **Operational Status**: [status.craft-sms.com]
- **Backup Window**: Automated point-in-time snapshots occur daily at 02:00 GMT.
